About Clumber Construction

The existing Clumber website.

Established in 2008 and based in Retford, Nottinghamshire. We carry out small to medium sized Civil Engineering and Construction projects throughout the Midlands and the North of England, as well as the rest of the UK, specialising in Infrastructure, Drainage, Highways, Earthworks and Industrial, Commercial and Private Developments..

We have achieved continued expansion as a result of quality schemes being delivered safely, on time and at increased value to our Clients. Our track record in delivering great operational performance has built long-term relationships with our clients, who rely on us to support their current needs and improve future outcomes.

Our Workforce is one of the most highly skilled and CSCS and CPCS training are compulsory across the board, along with training in specialist skills such as confined space working , Streetworks, Highway Works, Health and Safety and staff wellbeing.

We also invest heavily in our own plant, ensuring up-to-date performance and complete reliability.

We are an approved sub-contractor for Severn Trent Water, Yorkshire Water and Anglian Water, currently working on their AMP6 schemes for National Contractors.
